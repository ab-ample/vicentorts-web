• Vicent Orts
  • About

Vicent develops a design practice based on the questioning of the figure of the designer as the only creative entity capable of reaching useful and efficient solutions. Through the use of everyday objects and the decontextualization of these generates new elements with the intention of putting these ideas into practice and making the viewer reflect on them. To develop his practice he goes out in search of new objects, usually waste; A piece of cement, bricks, a PVC pipe are just some examples of objects that Vincent uses to question these ideas.
